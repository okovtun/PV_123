<p>As mentioned above, editing the scheme worked for me but getting to the scheme was different.</p>

<ul>
<li>Use the <strong>Product</strong> Menu</li>
<li>Select the <strong>Scheme</strong> option</li>
<li>Select the <strong>Edit Scheme</strong> option</li>
<li>Click the <strong>Options</strong> Tab</li>
<li>Tick the "<strong>Working Directory</strong>" item</li>
<li>Click the small icon at the right end of the text box.</li>
<li>Select the directory</li>
</ul>

<p>I also had the <strong>Run/Debug</strong> option selected in the left hand pane of the window.</p>
